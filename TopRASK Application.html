TopRASK Application

This Top RASK application is designed to displays demand seasonality and holiday-driven calendar shifts. Weekly and yearly seasonality components, as well as holiday influences, are learned and forecasted by the Prophet model. Events and holidays are detected with the help of Generative AI models. Demand Components:
Prophet builds its revenue forecast by combining a trend component (long-term growth/decline), holiday effects, and weekly/yearly seasonality.
Source: https://facebook.github.io/prophet/docs/seasonality,_holiday_effects,_and_regressors.html
Display Holidays:
Holidays are added as special events that can cause temporary changes in demand,
like drops during Christmas or spikes before school holidays.
This helps the model separate holiday effects from normal patterns.
To reflect their true importance, holidays are weighted by PoO LF contribution.

Events are not used for demand mapping. They are found by generative AI models, and are just for your info! Click on the events for more information.

Root Mean Squared Error
Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE) measures how far Predicted RASK values are from the Observed RASK
(Observed = Backtest RASK when available, otherwise Flown RASK).
It penalizes larger RASK errors more strongly, making it sensitive to outliers.
More info: statisticshowto.com
